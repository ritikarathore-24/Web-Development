Maggie Recipe

  1. Take a bowl and add some oil.
  2. Now add some chopped onion and green chills.
  3. After the onion become brown add the veggies as per your choice like:
    • Peas
    • Tomatos
    • Cauliflower
    • Carrot
    • Capsicum
  4. Now, after sauty the veggies add some spieces like:
    • Red chilli powder
    • Salt
    • Turmeric
    • Corrinder powder
    • Chat masala
    • Maggie masala
  5. After cooking the spieces, add 2 cups of water.
  6. When the water boiled, add the maggie cake.
  7. When the cake becoming melt, stir it.
  8. Serve the hot maggie in the bowl.
  9. Garnish the maggie with some fresh Corrinder leaves.
